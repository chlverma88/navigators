Need of the Project:

Many low-income families in areas like Govind Puri, Sangam Vihar, and Tughlakabad in Delhi desire to move to safer neighborhoods with better economic opportunities but face barriers such as lack of information, financial resources, and logistical support. The KITES project aims to empower adolescent girls as navigators to assist these families in overcoming these barriers and accessing better living conditions, while integrating STEM education and skills development.

About KITES

Katha Information Technology and Entrepreneurship School (KITES) has been working in Govindpuri for the past 24 years. KITES offers quality IT education to children as well as the youth from the communities to enhance opportunities for them. Working with British Telecommunications (BT), Katha has created a huge impact on children in slums.
