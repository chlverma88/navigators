Women Helpline (181, 1091)

The Women Helpline, accessible through the toll-free number 181, provides 24/7 emergency and non-emergency support to women facing violence or distress, offering referrals to relevant authorities and information about government schemes.

How to Access the Helpline:

  • Dial 181: Dial the toll-free number 181 from any phone.
  • Seek Assistance: Explain your situation to the helpline operator.
  • Receive Support: The operator will provide assistance and/or refer you to the appropriate resources.

Child Helpline

CHILDLINE 1098 is a phone number that spells hope for millions of children across India. It is a 24-hour a day, 365 days a year, free, emergency phone service for children in need of aid and assistance. We not only respond to the emergency needs of children but also link them to relevant services for their long-term care and rehabilitation. We have, till date, connected to three million children across the nation offering them care and protection.

CHILDLINE India Foundation (CIF) is the nodal agency of the Union Ministry of Women and Child Development for setting up, managing and monitoring the CHILDLINE 1098 service all over the country. CIF is the sole agency/body responsible for establishing the CHILDLINE service across the country, monitoring of service delivery and finance, training, research and documentation, creating awareness, advocacy as well as resource generation for the service.

Centralized Emergency Services (Dial: 112)

A centralized emergency services system, like the Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) in India, aims to provide a unified and efficient response to emergencies by consolidating emergency calls and dispatching resources through a single number, like 112.

Process Flow:

  • A citizen dials 112
  • The call is routed to the relevant PSAP
  • The PSAP agent takes the call and gathers information
  • The system displays pre-populated fields like location and caller details
  • The dispatcher dispatches the appropriate emergency vehicles
  • The emergency vehicles arrive at the scene
  • The call is closed after the incident is resolved

Disability Helpline

The National Disability Information Helpline Service (NDIHS), an initiative of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD), provides a telephonic information service on disabilities, accessible by calling 14456

How it works:

  • Purpose: To provide information, guidance, and counseling related to disability.
  • Accessibility: Accessible from any part of India by calling 14456.
  • Language: Information is available in English and Hindi.
  • Disabilities Covered: The helpline covers 21 disabilities as per the RPwD Act 2016.
  • Initiator: An initiative of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD).
